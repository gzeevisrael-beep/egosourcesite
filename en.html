The barrier is not something outside the person

It is revealed where personal benefit is more important than connection, and where a person cannot regard another's desire as one's own.

A person does not defeat nature by personal strength, but prepares the correct lack for the light that reforms.

The path passes through connection

1

Reveal the shattering

Do not cover the rejection or be ashamed of it. Recognize it as the place of work prepared for the person.

2

Receive importance from the environment

From the friends, a person receives the greatness of the goal, the importance of connection, and the power to go above personal reason.

3

Build a common lack

We do not ask for a pleasant feeling, but for the power to connect and make room for the quality of bestowal to be revealed among us.

4

Hand the work to the light

After every effort toward connection, a person recognizes that the correction itself is not in one's hands, but comes from the Source.

03

Three minutes of inner clarification

Building a common request

Choose your present state and move through three clarifications, without demanding that you correct nature by your own strength.

Clarification 1 of 3

Name the truth

The essential reversal

The individual does not cross the barrier alone.
Connection becomes the place where the light acts.

The ten is not merely a supportive environment, but a place for building a common vessel. Each person brings a lack, and together they direct it toward one goal: that the form of bestowal will dwell among them.
